our services

Offensive
Engagements

Trust is a good thing, but control is better specially if it comes to the security of mission critical IT systems. From attacker simulations on vast company networks to fine-tuned web application audits, we cover a wide area of topics where we help identify and remediate vulnerabilities and expose uncalculated risks.

SAP Enterprice
Security

By combining SAP best practices with a background in penetration testing, we developed our custom approach to realistically assess the security of SAP enterprise systems and applications. Identifying root causes and developing suitable mitigation measures while bringing together different stake holders is our key to a holistic approach for SAP security.

Defensive
Security

Since prevention is better than mitigation, we aid customers in assessing and implementing a security strategy before damage happens. To enhance operational security awareness workshops and guideline reviews help clients build up a robust and fault tolerance security. In case of a security incident we can help with Post Mortem analysis and recovery.

About Us

nullFaktor is an independent security and research company with an affinity for complex SAP environments. Our mission is to help customers identify and mitigate critical vulnerabilities in their systems starting all the way from the drawing board down to the core infrastructure.
